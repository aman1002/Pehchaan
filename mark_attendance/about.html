About Us

Pehchaan is a very efficient, fast, accurate, highly scalable and reliable cloud-based system to mark the attendance in a classroom in a single snapshot using face recognition and artificial intelligence that can be accessed using android mobile application and web application .


Advantages of this system

  • Saves precious study time by automatically marking attendance in few seconds
  • Highly automated process as it uses faces recognition
  • Highly scalable as it runs on cloud platform
  • Attendance can be taken repeatedly in every lecture
  • Less chances of error and automatic improvements through machine learning
  • No proxy attendance by students
  • Also records regularity of teachers
  • Can also be used to take attendance in offices by capturing images of employees at office gates
